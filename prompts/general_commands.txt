__General Commands__

``[general_key]getavatar (@)User``
    > Gets the Discord avatar for the user you mention.
``[general_key]flip``
    > Flips a coin and returns heads or tails.
``[general_key]roll #``
    > Rolls a #-sided dice. If no # given, assumes a 6-sided dice.
``[general_key]yesorno``
    > Tells you yes or no. Useful for making life decisions.
``[general_key]help``
    > Shows information.
``[general_key]commands``
    > Lists these commands.
